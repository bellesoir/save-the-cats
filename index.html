Did You Know?

A single pair of cats and their kittens can produce as many as 420,000 kittens in just 7 years!

Up A Tree

Squirrels have a joint in their hind feet that allows them to rotate their feet backwards so that they can hang onto a tree regardless of the orientation of their body. Cats can't do that. If they haven't learned to climb down backwards, they're stuck waiting for rescue.
